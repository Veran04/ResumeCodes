About Us

Supercharged Games was founded in 2018, as a way to express my love for gaming. While the tournaments described on this website and the video on the homepage are obviously not hosted by me or this company in reality, the love for videogames that is expressed by me in this project is very real. Additionally, I will attach some photographs of my real retro video game collection on the Services page when talking about buying and selling of video games. I have always had a passion for gaming, and it is what inevitably led me down my path to becoming a Computer Science major. Even graduating this December, I have never lost my love for video games and have expanded beyond just playing them to collecting them and improving their condition. Not only are they continuing to exponentially grow in value, I also just enjoy having them around to see in my house. I have also truly hosted and attented tournaments for Super Smash Bros Melee in the past, and was ranked before coming to UCF just outside of the top 100 in the world.

My mission with creating this website and company is to spread the love of video games to even more people. As I continue to accumulate more wealth, I wish to redistribute it in a way that will make people happy, and I think creating a central hub for people to play and trade video games at would be a great way to do so.
